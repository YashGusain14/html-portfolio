The Best Movies Of All Time according to Yash

My Top 3 Movies of All Time

Fight Club

This movie motivates me to be a man, focus on myself and take action.

Good Will Hunting

This movie made me realise that Education isn't the only path to brilliance and that facing and addressing emotions is critical for personal growth.

500 Days of Summer

This movie helped me realise the futility of the existing love-culture and that working on yourself and loving yourself is paramount to finding a good partner.
